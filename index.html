Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-12-27

This Privacy Policy explains how SwipeTidy (the “App”) handles information. The App is designed to process your photo library on-device.

1) Photos & Permissions

  • The App requests access to your Photos library to display items and let you decide what to keep or delete.
  • Photo analysis (duplicates, screenshots, blur detection, barcode detection) is performed on your device.
  • We do not upload your photos or videos to our servers.

2) Data We Collect

  • No account required and no server-side profile.
  • No analytics, no ads SDK, no third-party trackers.
  • No crash reporter SDK. (Apple may still provide crash/diagnostic data if you enabled sharing in iOS settings.)
  • Local app data only (on your device): preferences, language, scan cache, premium state, and similar operational data.
  • Purchases are processed by Apple (StoreKit). We do not receive your payment details.

3) Data Sharing

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your photos with third parties.
  • Apple may process purchase-related information as part of App Store transactions.

4) Retention

  • Local data remains on your device and can be removed by deleting the App.
